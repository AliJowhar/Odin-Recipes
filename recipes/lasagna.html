Lasagna

Prep Time:
35 Mins
Cook time
1 Hr 10 mins
Additional time
10 mins
Total time
1 Hr 55 mins
Servings
8
  • Add uncooked lasagna noodles right into this recipe for even quicker prep.
  • Home cooks enjoy the rich flavor and the flexibility to add extra
    cheese or sauce if they choose.
  • One Allrecipes member says, "The key to this recipe is that it must be
    covered tightly with foil."

Ingredients

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

Cheese Filling:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Other:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
